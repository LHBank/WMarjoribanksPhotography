Black and White Artworks

To those who perhaps think that Black and White photography lacks the excitement and vibrancy, an image bursting with color does. I think that's what Black and White Photography is supposed to do. To unleash your imagination. To build the story in your own head. To leave you with a sense of wanting more. Or at least it does for me.
